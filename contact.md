## Frequently Asked Questions

**How can I join MAPZ?**
: Visit our [Join Us](/join/) page for membership information and registration details.

**What events does MAPZ organize?**
: Check our [Events](/events/) page for upcoming activities and past highlights.

**I'm an alumnus, can I still participate?**
: Absolutely! MAPZ welcomes both current students and alumni. [Learn more](/about/).

: We organize regular meetups and events throughout the year. Follow us on social media for updates!
